About Me

I'm a software engineer and a writer currently based in Manhattan. I grew up in North Salem, NY, a small town an hour north of New York.

I studied at New York University for four years and I received my BA in Computer Science in 2018. I took a wide range of courses from User Experience Design to Science Fiction. Applied Internet Technology, Large Scale Web Applications, and Computer Networks were foundational courses for my interest in web technologies.

I love building web applications in JavaScript and learning more about web technologies. I've had some great internships and freelance work over the years, and I've enjoyed bolstering my skill set with programming projects such as Explore Spotify and Bike NYC. In July 2019, I'll be joining Google as a Cloud Technical Resident in their Austin, Texas office.

In addition to my academics and career focii, I love to read and write. When I was at NYU I worked for the Washington Square News and wrote articles about technology, policy, and culture. I love to read, especially science fiction as well as non-fiction about the human brain, technology, and happiness.
